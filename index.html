My Work

Welcome to Selah Jewellery, founded by Lynn-Lee Breytenbach in 2023, fueled by a deep love for what I do. A testament to elegance and sophistication. Our bespoke jewellery creations, crafted from the finest medals like silver and gold, we specialize in crafting jewellery adorned with a variety of diamonds, moissanites, precious and semi-precious gemstones. Embrace the journey with SELAH creating the perfect adornment for any occasion. We ensure that every SELAH piece is not just jewellery, but a timeless work of art destined to be treasured for generations.
